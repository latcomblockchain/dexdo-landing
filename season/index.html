Rules

Season rules

✓Season 1 runs on Shellnet (testnet). Points are provisional.
✓Rewards are paid in the NACKL token. On mainnet launch, points convert into a NACKL allocation.
✓To get your reward — claim at the end of the season. You withdraw to a public wallet and confirm ownership.
✓Exact point weights are private. That's anti-gaming protection. The list of actions and season rules are public.
✓Rules don't change retroactively against participants.
✓Gaming is filtered out. Self-trading and multi-accounts don't count — verified at claim.
✓Actions have limits — anti-bot.
